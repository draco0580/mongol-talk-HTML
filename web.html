FAQ

Common questions

Is the app free to use?

Mongol Talk is free to download with basic translation features. Premium features such as unlimited translations and adjustable voice speed are available through in-app subscription. Pricing is shown in the app before any purchase.

Which languages are supported?

We support Mongolian, Kazakh, English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Turkish, French, German, Spanish, and more. The complete list is available in the app.

How does voice translation work?

The app uses AI speech recognition to transcribe your voice, neural machine translation to convert the text, and text-to-speech to read the result aloud. All three steps happen within seconds.

How is my data handled?

Voice audio is sent to cloud AI services for translation processing. Translation history is stored locally on your device. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Changes take effect at the end of the current billing period.
